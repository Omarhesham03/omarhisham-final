Manga   are Japanese comic books or graphic novels. The majority of manga follow to a style created in Japan in the late nineteenth century, but the genre has a lengthy history in previous Japanese art. In Japan, the word manga refers to both comics and cartooning. Outside of Japan, the term is mainly used to refer to Japanese-published comics.
